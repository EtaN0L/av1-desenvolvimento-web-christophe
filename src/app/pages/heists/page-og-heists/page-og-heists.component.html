Original Apartment Heists

There are 5 Apartment heists,

  1. Fleeca Heist - 2 players
  2. Prison Break - 4 players
  3. Humane Labs Heist - 4 players
  4. Series A - 4 players
  5. Pacific Standard Heist - 4 players

It is required to own a high range appartment, be above level 20 and have met Lester Crest to be able to start these heists as leader.
